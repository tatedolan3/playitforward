Our Story

Built by players. For players.

Where It Started

Overwhelming. Random.
Lucky.

In 2019, our two founders started their freshman year at UC Berkeley on the men's soccer team. They both came from southern California, they played in the same club league, and they had similar academic backgrounds. However, while they shared the same destination at Cal, their paths looked nothing alike.

It took them years to realize how much luck was involved in each of their processes. Their access to reliable information about the recruiting process was non-existent. While that information is out there today, it is locked up in places that cost hefty sums of money for second-hand opinions.

Our founders asked a simple but unexplored question:

"Coaches are the ones who actually make the decisions about recruiting. Why do they not have a platform to educate those looking to play at the next level?"

From that question, Play It Forward was born. Now, we have made it our mission to provide you with access to quality information directly from the source on how to navigate the recruiting process.

Why We Built This

Skip the middleman.
Get it straight from the source.

Play It Forward exists to create the primary market for college recruiting information. On-demand content, built directly with active college coaches, giving athletes an unfiltered look at exactly how programs think, what they look for, and how to stand out.

No consultants. No markups. No oracle holding your hand and charging you for the privilege. Just knowledge — the real kind — delivered directly from the people whose opinions actually matter.

We want every athlete who comes to this platform to walk away feeling something specific: empowered. Not dependent. Not anxious. Not at the mercy of some expensive gatekeeper. Armed with real information, real tools, and the confidence to grab the recruiting process on their own terms.

You don't need someone to do it for you. You just need to know what you're doing. That's what we're here for.
